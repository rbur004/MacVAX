H_floating and Oct Instructions

    This group of instructions may be omitted from the VAX instruction set. Attempted execution of an omitted Instruction will cause a reserved instruction fault. MacVAX Omits this group.

    ACBH Add Compare and Branch H Float omitted
    ADDH2 Add H Float (2 arg) omitted
    ADDH3 Add H Float (3 arg) omitted
    CLRH (CLRO) Clear H Float (Clear Octa) omitted
    CMPH Compare H Float omitted
    CVTBH Convert Byte to H Float omitted
    CVTDH Convert Double Float to H Float omitted
    CVTFH Convert Float to H Float omitted
    CVTGH Convert G Float to H Float omitted
    CVTHB Convert H Float to Byte omitted
    CVTHD Convert H Float to Double Float omitted
    CVTHF Convert H Float to Float omitted
    CVTHG Convert H Float to G Float omitted
    CVTHL Convert H Float to Long omitted
    CVTHW Convert H Float to Word omitted
    CVTLH Convert Long to H Float omitted
    CVTRHL Convert H Float (rounded) to Long omitted
    CVTWH Convert Word to H Float omitted
    DIVH2 Divide H Float (2 arg) omitted
    DIVH3 Divide H Float (3 arg) omitted
    EMODH Extended Multiply and Integerize H Float omitted
    MNEGH Move Negated H Float omitted
    MOVAH (MOVAO) Move Addredd of H Float (or Octa) omitted
    MOVH Move H Float omitted
    MOVO Move Octa omitted
    MULH2 Multiply H Float (2 arg) omitted
    MULH3 Multiply H Float (3 arg) omitted
    PUSHAH (PUSHAO) Push Address H Float (or Octa) omitted
    POLYH Polynomial Evaluation H Float omitted
    SUBH2 Subtract H Float (2 arg) omitted
    SUBH3 Subtract H Float (3 arg) omitted
    TSTH test H Float omitted
