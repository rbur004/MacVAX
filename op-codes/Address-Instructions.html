Address Instructions

    MOVAB Move Address of Byte
    MOVAW Move Address of Word
    MOVAL Move Address of Long
    MOVAQ Move Address of Quad
    MOVAF Move Address of Float
    MOVAD Move Address of Double
    MOVAH (MOVAO) Move Addredd of H Float (or Octa) omitted

    PUSHAB Push Address of Byte onto Stack
    PUSHAW Push Address of Word onto Stack
    PUSHAL Push Address of Long onto Stack
    PUSHAQ Push Address of Quad onto Stack
    PUSHAF Push Address of Float onto Stack
    PUSHAD Push Address of Double onto Stack
