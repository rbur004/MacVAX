G_floating Instructions

    This group of instructions may be omitted from the VAX instruction set. Attempted execution of an omitted Instruction will cause a reserved instruction fault. MacVAX Omits this group.

    ACBG Add Compare and Branch G Float omitted
    ADDG2 Add G Float (2 arg) omitted
    ADDG3 Add G Float (3 arg) omitted
    CMPG Compare G Float omitted
    CVTBG Convert Byte to G Float omitted
    CVTFG Convert Float to G Float omitted
    CVTGB Convert G Float to Byte omitted
    CVTGF Convert G Float to Float omitted
    CVTGL Convert G Float to Long omitted
    CVTGW Convert G Float to Word omitted
    CVTLG Convert Long to G Float omitted
    CVTRGL Convert G Float (rounded) to Long omitted
    CVTWG Convert Word to G Float omitted
    DIVG2 Divide G Float (2 arg) omitted
    DIVG3 Divide G Float (3 arg) omitted
    EMODG Extended Multiply and Integerize G Float omitted
    MNEGG Move Negated G Floatomitted
    MOVG Move G Float omitted
    MULG2 Multiply G Float (2 arg) omitted
    MULG3 Multiply G Float (3 arg) omitted
    POLYG Polynamial evaluation G Float omitted
    SUBG2 Subtract G Float (2 arg) omitted
    SUBG3 Subtract G Float (3 arg) omitted
    TSTG Test G Float omitted
